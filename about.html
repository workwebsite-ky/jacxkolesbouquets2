The story

Behind every Jacxkole’s bouquet is one person, a pair of careful hands, and a love for turning ordinary moments into something unforgettable.

By Ja’Cole Payne · “Miss Flower Girl”

A girl who wanted to turn simple moments into something special

Ja’Cole is a professional, personable and passionate florist and crafter who loves to spread love, joy and kindness through floral and craft work.

She loves to create, inspire and re-create. What started as a passion for providing others with thoughtfully crafted bouquets grew into a brand built on attention, care, and a desire to make every person feel seen, celebrated and special. You imagine it… she creates it.

Proudly established in 2024 in Saint Petersburg, Florida — and growing one handmade bouquet at a time.

Mission

Beauty that lasts

To handcraft bouquets that capture emotion — from romantic gestures to everyday self-love — and to make every recipient feel celebrated.

Vision

Every bouquet, a story

To become the go-to floral and craft house where your imagination is the only limit — locally and far beyond St. Pete.
